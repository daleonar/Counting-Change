Counting Change

Input an amount in cents and click 'Get Change.' You will get every possible way to to get that value in pennies, nickels, dimes, quarters, and half-dollars.

Don't go too high. Like Icarus, you may crash... your browser.

200 is probably the highest you should go. Even then, there's a heart stopping moment in which it's unclear if it's struggling or if you outright broke it.
